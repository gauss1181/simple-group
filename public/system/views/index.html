Arranging meetups with friends has never been easier.

Simply enter a post indicating how you're feeling or what you feel like doing at the moment, and through the power of natural language processing, the app will match you with a meetup group to hang out with based on the activity you feel like doing at the moment or based on what activity seems best to enlighten your current emotions.

Created by Megan Chen at HackSummit 2014.
Technologies: AngularJS, Node.js, MongoDB, Google Maps API
